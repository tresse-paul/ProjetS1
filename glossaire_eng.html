Glossary

Find here the definitions of complex words.

Feminism :
Feminism regroups several movements promoting gender equality, in politics, economics or even social and cultural.

SPD :
This is the German social democratic party which appeared under this name in 1875.

Ideology :
A set of ideas specific to a culture that helps to guide movements, it political, religious, economic or other.

Suffragettes :
A group of feminists fighting for the right to vote in England.

Claim :
To impose himself in a strong way to claim one's rights.

Patriarchy :
A state of mind where the man holds the authority, he defines himself as unique and superior.
